Reed Solomon performance test on a PIC32 with encoding of 200 full RS(223, 255) packets:

At 40 MHz:
Encoding: 2687 ms total, 13.4 ms per packet.
Decoding: 3869 ms total, 19.3 ms per packet.

At 80 MHz:
Encoding: 1390 ms total, 6.95 ms per packet.
Decoding: 2137 ms total, 10.68 ms per packet.

This gives a maximum bit rate of 47940 bps.
